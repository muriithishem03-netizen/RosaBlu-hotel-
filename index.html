The Restaurant & Coffee Shop

Home of our world-famous Jamaican Jerk Chicken — a well-seasoned, grilled chicken with an authentic marinade providing the perfect balance of heat and sweetness, served with crispy French fries. Amazingly tender, moist, and juicy in every single bite!

The Bar, Lounge & Terrace

Unwind under warm, golden industrial statement lighting in our custom open-air wooden terrace lounge. The perfect spot for a cold drink or a freshly brewed specialty coffee. An atmosphere unlike anything else in Kirinyaga.

Local Gateway Excursions

Your ideal launchpad for exploring Kirinyaga County. Located just an 18-minute drive from the Kerugoya Urban Forest and Kerugoya Stadium, and only 46 minutes from the majestic Mount Kenya National Park. Our team can help arrange transport and guided excursions upon request.
